# AI-powered-SEO-content-production

## Overview

This repository contains research on **AI-powered SEO content production**.

The objective of this project is to study how leading SEO professionals, AI marketers, and content strategists use artificial intelligence to improve content creation, search visibility, and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

---

## Repository Structure

```
research/
├── sources.md
├── linkedin-posts/
├── youtube-transcripts/
└── other/
```

---

## What I Collected

This repository includes:

- A curated list of 10 experts in AI-powered SEO content production
- Recent LinkedIn posts organized by author
- YouTube transcripts where publicly available
- Additional research materials and notes

---

## Why These Experts?

The selected experts actively share practical knowledge and real-world experience in:

- AI-powered SEO
- Content production workflows
- AI Search
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
- SEO automation
- AI tools for content marketing

They were chosen because they consistently publish valuable insights that help understand how AI is transforming modern SEO and content strategies.

---

## Collection Method

- LinkedIn posts were manually collected from each expert's public profile.
- YouTube transcripts were collected using publicly available transcript sources where available.
- All research materials were organized into dedicated folders for easier navigation and reference.
