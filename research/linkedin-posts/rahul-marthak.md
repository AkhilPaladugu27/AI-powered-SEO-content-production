how i got a fresh website into chatgpt answers in 7 days (and why it matters)

day one: nobody knew my client existed. they had a fresh website. decent content. zero authority. zero presence anywhere. search console was a ghost town.

but i noticed something. medical students were going to chatgpt asking exam questions. chatgpt was giving answers. but the sources were scattered. outdated. incomplete.

day two and three: i didn't build links. i didn't optimize for google. instead, i created something chatgpt actually wanted. a comprehensive page that answered the exact question students were asking. structured clearly. cited properly. real, organized information that an ai could parse and recommend without hesitation. no fluff. no keyword stuffing. just pure utility formatted in a way that made sense to a language model.

day four: i made sure the page was accessible. technical foundation tight. fast load. clean html.

day five: i submitted it to the places where students actually asked these questions. forums. communities.

day six: chatgpt started citing it. then recommending it. then pulling from it in answers.

day seven: the client's fresh website was showing up in ai-generated responses to medical exam questions.

here's what nobody talks about: seo and aeo are not the same game anymore. google wants links and authority and brand signals. ai models want clarity and structure and actual answers. the client didn't win at seo in a week. they won at aeo.

and aeo is faster. cleaner. more direct. because it rewards utility over politics.

the best part? once chatgpt starts recommending you, google notices. the traffic that follows is different. it's not volume chasing. it's actual students who need actual help finding an actual resource.

**Annotation:** Rahul's day-by-day case study of getting a zero-authority website cited by ChatGPT within one week is one of the most concrete AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) execution examples available, distinguishing clearly between what Google rewards (authority, backlinks) vs. what AI models reward (clarity, structure, direct answers). This case study is directly applicable to AI-powered SEO content production methodologies and serves as a compelling proof-of-concept for the "AEO-first" content strategy.
