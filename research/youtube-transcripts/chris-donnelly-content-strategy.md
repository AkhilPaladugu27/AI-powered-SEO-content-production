## Summary

Chris Donnelly presents eight content strategies for businesses that are struggling with outdated approaches. He argues that most businesses are stuck in legacy tactics and outlines a framework including **Problem Matching** (opening content with the customer's pain point, not the brand's credentials), **"Blank for Blank" Positioning** (e.g. "the Stripe for charities"), and **Agitate the Problem** (deepening emotional resonance before offering solutions). A major section covers **Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO)** — structuring content so AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity can directly cite your business — which he calls the most under-exploited channel in 2026. He also advocates for **Founder-Led Personal Branding** as a compounding asset that makes every future product launch easier, and **Educational Content** as the best long-term trust builder.
