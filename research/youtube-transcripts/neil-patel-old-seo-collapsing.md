## Summary

Neil Patel declares the end of the "rank #1 and win" paradigm that defined SEO for 20 years, citing his agency NP Digital's data showing rankings holding steady while organic clicks drop sharply — especially on queries touched by AI Overviews. He introduces the replacement framework: a **multi-surface presence strategy** where brands must be visible in AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Reddit, YouTube, and TikTok simultaneously. Key tactics include building **brand entity authority** (so AI models recognise your brand as a trusted source), generating **digital PR and third-party mentions** (which he calls the #1 SEO advantage in 2026), and creating content that satisfies **conversational query formats** rather than keyword-stuffed articles. He warns that companies relying purely on Google rankings are already losing ground to competitors who have diversified their discovery channels.
