## Summary

Anastasia Kotsiubynska shares findings from SE Ranking's large-scale AI Overviews study (tracking 100,000+ keywords). AI Overviews have grown approximately 5% across the keyword set they tracked — which sounds small but represents a massive absolute volume. She notes that AI Overviews threaten organic SEO visibility significantly: when an AI Overview appears, the organic results below it see substantially reduced click-through rates. She highlights that Reddit and Quora links are notably absent from AI Overview citations, contrary to what many SEOs expected. A key concern is that AI is now handling sensitive queries in legal and healthcare verticals without adequate disclaimers, which she argues requires regulatory barriers. She also discusses how clients are urgently requesting metrics that track the traffic impact of AI Overviews, signalling a shift in how SEO success is measured.
